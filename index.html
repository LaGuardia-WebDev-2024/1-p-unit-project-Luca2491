My Camera

There are two main parts to a DSLR camera.

  • The body
    • This part of the camera is what holds the image sensor, the viewfinder, and the screen to view your images. It is what actually takes the photo.
  • The lens
    • Without the lens it is impossible to take a clear photo. It is what directs the light from outside directly onto the sensor.

    There are many different types of lenses.
    • a zoom lens
    • a prime lens
