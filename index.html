My Camera

There are two main parts to a DSLR camera.

  • The body
    • This part of the camera is what holds the image sensor, the viewfinder, and the screen to view your images. It is what actually takes the photo.
  • The lens
    • Without the lens it is impossible to take a clear photo. It is what directs the light from outside directly onto the sensor.

    There are many different types of lenses. the two main categories are:
    • zoom lens:
      • a zoom lens is able to zoom in and out. this is called the focal distance and it is measured in millimeters. For example, a very common lens new photographers will buy is a 24-70mm lens. 24 being the widest field of view it can go and 70 being the narrowest. it is wide enough for landscapes and can zoom in far enough for portraits or wildlife photography.
